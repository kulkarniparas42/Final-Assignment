About Us

Frullato Jalgaon began as a small fruit stand in 2018 and has since blossomed into the town's favorite spot for handcrafted shakes and desserts. Rooted in quality, community and flavor, we bring you seasonal fruits, roasted nuts and innovative blends in every cup.

🍃 Our Mission

Delivering joy and refreshment through premium, handcrafted beverages.

🛠️ Our Process

We source fresh, local produce, roast our nuts in-house, and blend to perfection—every time.

💖 Our Values

Quality ingredients, community engagement and sustainable practices.
